Rectangle Collision

An interactive visualization of colliding rectangles.

For two rectangles to collide all the following conditions must hold true at the same time.

  • A's right edge to the right of B's left edge.
  • A's left edge to the left of B's right edge.
  • A's bottom edge below B's top edge.
  • A's top edge above B's bottom edge.

If the opposite (De Morgan) holds true there is no collision. In other words if at least one of the following conditions is true.

  • A's right edge to the left of B's left edge.
  • A's left edge to the right of B's right edge.
  • A's bottom edge above B's top edge.
  • A's top edge below B's bottom edge.
